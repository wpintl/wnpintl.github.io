Who we are

USPTO Patent agents

Based mainly in the U.S., we are patent agents registered with the United States Patent and Trademark Office.

We have extensive patent prosecution experience across many technological fields. We have counseled numerous clients on matters concerning: patent portfolio acquisition and management; patent portfolio licenses, sales and purchases; joint development agreements; freedom to practice opinions; patentability opinions; non-infringement opinions; and enforceability opinions.

We are dedicated to serve our clients to file patent applications in USPTO as well as other countries, such as China.

With our language skills, we can help answering patent-related questions regardless if you are based in U.S. or China. Our service includes conducting novelty searches and providing opinions that include U.S. and China patent or non-patent literatures.
